ABOUT WEBTECH CORE

We're the growth team small businesses wish they had in-house.

WebTech Core was built on a simple belief: small businesses deserve the same marketing firepower as the big brands — without the bloated retainers or the jargon.

// OUR STORY

Started in a garage. Built for Main Street.

We began as two marketers frustrated watching great local businesses lose to competitors who simply marketed better. So we set out to level the field.

Today, WebTech Core runs the full marketing engine for hundreds of small businesses across India and the US — SEO, ads, social, web, and content under one roof, with one accountable team.
