// A MARKET ON THE RISE

Bihar's growing tech market

Bihar has long exported talent to India's biggest tech companies — now that energy is coming home. Patna is emerging as a regional technology hub, supported by a state startup policy, new co-working and incubation spaces, and a wave of young founders building for Bharat. Smartphones, cheap data, and UPI have collapsed the distance between a small-town shop and a national audience.
