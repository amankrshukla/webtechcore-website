Frequently asked questions about digital marketing for small businesses

How much does digital marketing cost for a small business in India?+

Digital marketing pricing for small businesses in India ranges from ₹8,000/month for basic SEO to ₹50,000+/month for full-service packages including SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, and social media management. WebTech Core offers starter packages from ₹9,999/month with no long-term contracts required.

How long does SEO take to show results in India?+

Most small businesses see measurable SEO results — keyword ranking improvements and organic traffic growth — within 90 to 180 days. Local SEO targeting city-specific searches on Google Maps can show results in 30 to 60 days when the Google Business Profile is fully optimized.

What is the difference between SEO and Google Ads for small businesses?+

SEO builds long-term, free organic visibility on Google that compounds over time but takes 3–6 months. Google Ads delivers immediate traffic and leads from day one but stops when you stop paying. For most small businesses, the highest-ROI strategy combines both: Ads for immediate leads, SEO for long-term cost reduction.

Can WebTech Core manage both India and USA digital marketing campaigns?+

Yes. WebTech Core operates across India (all 29 states) and the USA (all 50 states). Indian campaigns are managed with India-specific keyword behavior, local citation sites, and Indian search intent patterns. US campaigns follow separate market research and American search intent.

Yes. Starter packages begin at ₹9,999/month in India or $299/month in the USA. Every engagement starts with a free 30-minute strategy call and a written audit report delivered within 48 hours — before you spend anything.
