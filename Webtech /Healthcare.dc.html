// COMPLIANCE & ETHICS

Healthcare marketing that stays compliant — NMC, DPDPA & YMYL

Healthcare marketing operates under regulatory and ethical constraints that generic agencies routinely violate. We build every campaign within these guardrails from day one.

⚖️

NMC Advertising Guidelines

The NMC restricts individual doctor advertising. We promote your practice and its service capabilities — “LASIK surgery available, book a consultation,” never “guaranteed 20/20 vision.”

🔍

YMYL (Your Money Your Life)

Google holds all healthcare content to the highest E-E-A-T scrutiny. We build content with doctor review attributions, medical citations, disclaimers, and structured data quality raters expect.

🔒

DPDPA 2023 — Patient Privacy

Patient data from forms, bookings, and WhatsApp must comply with India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act. We build lead capture with DPDPA-compliant consent and data handling.

Frequently asked questions — healthcare digital marketing

How much does digital marketing cost for a clinic or hospital in India?+

For clinics and individual practices, it typically ranges from ₹15,000 to ₹50,000/month for GBP, local SEO, review management, and Google Ads. Hospitals and multi-specialty centers with department-level campaigns typically run ₹50,000–₹2,00,000/month. WebTech Core offers healthcare starter packages from ₹14,999/month with a free 48-hour audit before you commit.

What is the most important digital marketing channel for a doctor or clinic?+

Google Business Profile (Google Maps) is the single most important asset. Most ‘doctor near me’ and ‘clinic near me’ searches convert directly from the Maps listing. A fully optimized GBP with complete information, real photos, and recent reviews consistently drives the most booked appointments.

Is healthcare advertising allowed on Google and Meta in India?+

Yes, with restrictions. Healthcare advertising must follow platform medical guidelines and Indian advertising regulations — certain claims, before/after imagery, and prescription content are restricted. We build campaigns that are fully compliant while still effective, so your practice grows without policy or ethical risk.

How do I get more patient reviews for my practice?+

The most effective method is a systematic request sent shortly after a positive visit — via WhatsApp or SMS with a direct Google review link. Timing and ease are critical. We build this process into every engagement, helping practices consistently grow recent, genuine reviews that drive new patient trust.

How long does healthcare SEO take to show results?+

Google Business Profile optimization typically shows local-pack improvements within 30–60 days. Website and treatment-page SEO shows results within 90–180 days. Google Ads generate patient inquiries within the first 1–2 weeks — which is why we often run paid for immediate appointments while SEO builds long-term, lower-cost visibility.

Does WebTech Core serve healthcare providers across India and the USA?+

Yes. We work with clinics, hospitals, dental and specialty practices, diagnostic labs, and wellness providers across all major Indian cities and growth markets, and with healthcare practices across all 50 states of the USA — with compliant, market-specific strategies for each.
