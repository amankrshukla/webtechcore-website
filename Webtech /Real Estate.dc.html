// COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently asked questions — real estate digital marketing

How much does digital marketing cost for a real estate agent or builder in India?+

For agents and brokerages, it typically ranges from ₹15,000 to ₹50,000/month for GBP, local SEO, and Meta lead campaigns. For builders running project launches, performance campaigns plus a microsite typically run ₹50,000–₹2,00,000/month plus ad spend, scaled to the launch. WebTech Core offers real estate starter packages from ₹14,999/month with a free 48-hour audit before you commit.

How do I get qualified property leads instead of time-wasters?+

Lead quality comes from intent-based targeting and qualifying funnels. We target active property searchers (not broad interest audiences), add qualifying questions on lead forms (budget, location, timeline), and route only filtered leads to you. This dramatically raises the share of leads that actually visit and buy.

Are portals like 99acres and MagicBricks enough for real estate leads?+

Portals deliver volume, but the same lead is shared with multiple competitors and costs rise every year. The most successful agents and builders use portals for reach while building their own inbound channel — Google, Meta, SEO, and WhatsApp — where the lead is exclusively theirs and the cost per qualified lead is lower over time.

How long does real estate SEO take to show results?+

Google Business Profile and local SEO typically show local-pack improvements within 30–60 days. Competitive ‘property in [city]’ keywords take 90–180 days. Google and Meta lead campaigns generate inquiries within the first 1–2 weeks — which is why we usually run paid for immediate leads while SEO builds the long-term, lower-cost channel.

Can you help with a new project or property launch?+

Yes. For launches we build a dedicated project microsite, geo-targeted Google and Meta campaigns, walkthrough and RERA content, and an instant inquiry-to-site-visit booking flow — all tracked to booked site visits, the metric that matters for a launch.

Does WebTech Core serve real estate businesses across India and the USA?+

Yes. We work with agents, brokerages, builders, and property managers across all major Indian cities and growth markets, and with real estate professionals across all 50 states of the USA — with market-specific search, portal, and ad strategies for each.
