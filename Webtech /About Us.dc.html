ABOUT WEBTECH CORE

We are the growth team small businesses wish they had in-house.

WebTech Core was built on a single, non-negotiable belief: small businesses deserve the same marketing firepower as the big brands — without the bloated retainers, the jargon-filled reports, or the 12-month lock-in contracts that go nowhere.

We are a performance-driven digital marketing agency operating across all 29 states of India and all 50 states of the United States — built specifically to serve the small businesses that power local economies.

// OUR STORY

Started small. Built to level the field.

WebTech Core was founded in 2024 by a team of digital marketing professionals who shared the same frustration: watching genuinely great local businesses lose customers to bigger competitors — not because they offered a worse product, but simply because the bigger brand marketed better.

The dental clinic in Patna with no Google presence. The D2C founder in Surat whose product beats anything on Amazon but can't crack Meta Ads. The contractor in Texas with excellent reviews whose phone stays quiet because he ranks on page 4.

We built WebTech Core to fix that — to give every small business the complete marketing engine (SEO, ads, social, web, and content) that used to be reserved for enterprise budgets.

Today we run the full marketing operation for 250+ small businesses across India and the USA. One accountable team. Every channel. Real results reported every month in plain language.
