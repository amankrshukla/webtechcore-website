// THE ZOMATO & SWIGGY PROBLEM

The commission problem — and how we help you solve it

Zomato and Swiggy are powerful discovery platforms — but at 15–30% commission on every order, they're also your most expensive marketing channel. A restaurant doing ₹5,00,000 in monthly delivery revenue pays ₹75,000–₹1,50,000 in commissions alone.

The solution isn't to leave aggregators — it's to build a parallel direct ordering channel that grows alongside them. Every customer who orders through your website or WhatsApp is zero commission and 100% margin.

What we build for you

✓A direct ordering website with Razorpay/UPI — commission-free.
✓A WhatsApp ordering system with automated confirmation and tracking.
✓A customer database you own — names, numbers, and order history.
✓WhatsApp broadcasts that drive repeat orders, no platform dependency.
✓Google Ads sending “order [cuisine] online” traffic to your site, not Zomato.

The math

A restaurant with ₹3 lakh in monthly Zomato orders pays ₹45,000–₹90,000 in commissions. Shift just 30% of those orders to direct channels and you save ₹13,500–₹27,000 every month — that pays for your entire digital marketing budget, and then some.

// COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently asked questions — restaurant digital marketing

How much does digital marketing cost for a restaurant or cafe in India?+

It typically ranges from ₹12,000 to ₹50,000/month depending on scope. A basic package with GBP, Instagram, and review management starts from ₹12,000/month; full-service including Google/Meta ads, content shoots, and loyalty campaigns runs ₹30,000–₹50,000+/month. WebTech Core offers restaurant starter packages from ₹12,999/month with a free 48-hour audit.

What is the most important digital marketing channel for a restaurant?+

Google Business Profile and Instagram together. GBP drives ‘restaurants near me’ discovery and reservations; Instagram drives crave-ability and brand. A fully optimized GBP with great food photos, current hours and menu, and recent reviews is the single biggest driver of new footfall.

How do I reduce my dependence on Swiggy and Zomato commissions?+

Build a direct-ordering channel. We optimize your GBP and website for direct orders and reservations, build WhatsApp ordering, and run campaigns that drive customers to order direct — keeping the 18–25% you’d otherwise pay in aggregator commission, and giving you the customer data you don’t get from the platforms.

How do I get more Google reviews for my restaurant?+

Through a simple, well-timed system — QR codes on tables and bills, plus follow-up requests — that make it effortless for happy diners to leave a Google review right after a great meal. We build this into every engagement so you consistently grow recent, genuine reviews.

How long does restaurant marketing take to show results?+

GBP and Instagram improvements drive noticeable footfall within 30–60 days. Google and Meta ads can drive reservations and orders within the first 1–2 weeks. Local SEO for competitive ‘best restaurant in [area]’ terms builds over 90–180 days. We usually run paid for immediate covers while organic builds.

Does WebTech Core serve restaurants across India and the USA?+

Yes. We work with restaurants, cafes, cloud kitchens, bakeries, bars, and multi-outlet chains across all major Indian cities and growth markets, and with food businesses across all 50 states of the USA — with market-specific strategies for each.
