// THE URGENCY ADVANTAGE

‘Near me’ means ‘right now’ — the home services search urgency advantage

Home services is categorically different. When someone searches ‘plumber near me’, they don’t have days to research — their pipe is actively leaking. ‘Emergency electrician’ at 11 PM means they need help tonight. ‘AC repair Hyderabad’ in May means their apartment is 38 degrees.

This urgency creates the highest-intent, most conversion-ready traffic in all of local search. No consideration stage, no comparison shopping, no 3-week research window. Just: who shows up first and looks trustworthy? That’s who gets the call.

✓Google Maps top-3 position for every service + city you serve — because 55% of users click one of the first 3 results.

✓100+ Google reviews averaging 4.7★ or higher — because 72% of homeowners choose based on reviews and website quality combined.

// WHAT WE FIX

The real problems home service businesses face online

01

Not in the Google Maps top 3 — invisible when it matters

When a homeowner searches ‘electrician near me’, they see three map results and call one. Positions 4–10 are effectively invisible — not because they’re less skilled, but because their GBP is incomplete, reviews are outdated, or citations are inconsistent. We fix every factor systematically.

02

Feast-or-famine booking calendar

Fully booked in summer, empty in winter. Packed midweek, quiet on weekends. We build year-round lead flow with seasonal planning, off-peak Google Ads targeting, and maintenance reminders to past customers that fill the calendar in your slowest months.

03

Paying 10–20% commission to Urban Company per job

Urban Company generates bookings — but at 10–20% commission, no customer contact details, and constant suspension risk. We build your own direct booking channel through Google Maps and WhatsApp that generates commission-free jobs and a database you own permanently.

04

No review system — old or low reviews costing you jobs

72% of homeowners choose based on Google reviews. A plumber with 12 reviews at 4.1★ loses jobs daily to a competitor with 180 reviews at 4.8★. We build an automated review process that generates 10–20 new reviews per month from completed jobs.

05

Website that doesn’t work on mobile or have click-to-call

82% of home service searches happen on mobile. A customer with a burst pipe who can’t tap to call will close the tab and call the next result. We build fast, mobile-first sites with click-to-call on every page, instant WhatsApp, and a booking form above the fold.

06

No system to re-book past customers

Acquiring a new customer costs 5x more than re-booking an existing one. Most businesses have no system to remind past customers about seasonal servicing. We build WhatsApp and email reminder sequences that keep your name top-of-mind when they need their AC serviced again.
