// COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently asked questions — legal & professional services marketing

How much does digital marketing cost for a law firm or CA practice in India?+

It typically ranges from ₹20,000 to ₹75,000+/month depending on scope and competition. A foundation package with website, SEO, and content authority starts from ₹20,000/month; full-service including Google Ads, LinkedIn, and lead nurture runs ₹40,000–₹75,000+/month. WebTech Core offers professional services packages from ₹19,999/month with a free 48-hour audit.

Is digital advertising allowed for lawyers and CAs in India?+

It is restricted. Bar Council rules limit direct solicitation by advocates, and professional bodies have their own guidelines. We build compliant strategies — focused on authority content, SEO, and informational visibility rather than prohibited solicitation — so your firm grows ethically and within the rules. We tailor the approach to your profession’s specific guidelines.

What is the most effective channel for professional services marketing?+

Authority-led SEO and content combined with a credible website. High-value clients research extensively and choose the firm that appears most expert and trustworthy. Ranking for practice-area searches and publishing genuinely useful content consistently generates the highest-quality inquiries, supported by LinkedIn for B2B and Google for high-intent searches.

How do I get more high-value clients instead of low-value inquiries?+

Through intent and authority. We target specific, high-value practice-area searches (not broad terms), build content that pre-qualifies and educates, and add qualifying steps to inquiry forms. This filters for the serious, high-value clients and reduces time wasted on unqualified leads.

How long does SEO take to work for a law firm or consultancy?+

Professional services SEO typically shows meaningful ranking and inquiry growth within 3–6 months, as authority and content build. Google Ads (where permitted) and LinkedIn campaigns generate qualified inquiries within the first few weeks — so we often run paid for immediate leads while authority SEO compounds over time.

Does WebTech Core serve professional firms across India and the USA?+

Yes. We work with law firms, CA and CS practices, consultants, financial advisors, and other professional service providers across all major Indian cities and growth markets, and across all 50 states of the USA — with compliant, market-specific strategies for each.
